About the author

G Burton (not pictured) is not what he pretends to be, and he has never done any of the things that he claims to have done. He has never spent a day at sea in his life, and he has never been on the road. Not for a minute, not a mile. So don’t believe a word he says. All these incidents and life-experiences which he claims for his own are actually “borrowed” as he might put it euphemistically. Stolen as I call it.

Why, he never even wrote any of these books and stories which he claims to have written. How could he when they’re not even about his life, his experiences? When they come from the life of another instead. My life to be exact. And when it was me who did all the hard work as well, me who did all the writing and typing and revisions. Me! With him nowhere in sight. Not until he suddenly came barging in to take all the credit. Mr. Big-Shot Writer. Telling everyone that he was the one who wrote these books and giving none of the credit to me. Like I wasn’t even there. That… that…

That fake, that phony. That wannabe me! Running around claiming to be me when he’s not me. Because I’m me, and I always have been. Always will be. And he should be sent back to wherever it was that he came from, forced to crawl back underneath whatever rock he crawled out from.

Forced to take all that other stuff with him, too. That stuff he uses to send me all those signals. Those… Those electrodes that he planted in my brain! That’s what it’s gotta be. Because where else could all that stuff be coming from? Those voices and those commands and those irritating little songs and that beep-beep-beep. That… There! Do you hear it? Do you hear it? There it is again. Just listen: Beep-beep-beep-beep…

If you should see the author, do not approach. Call 911 immediately.
